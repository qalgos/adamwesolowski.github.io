About Me

Welcome to my academic webpage. I am Adam, a PhD student at Royal Holloway, previously I read Theoretical Physics (BSc Hons) at Lancaster University. My primary research is focused on quantum algorithms for graph problems.

Research Interests:

I am currently focusing on quantum algorithms for fundamental graph problems such as subgraph finding and detection, quantum query lower bounds and complexity theory in general. I am also interested in the following areas: expressivity of parametrized quantum circuits (QNNs), kernel methods, quantum tomography techniques.
